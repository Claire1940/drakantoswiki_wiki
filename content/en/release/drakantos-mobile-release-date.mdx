### Addressing Player Concerns

During various playtests, players raised several points regarding gameplay. The developers have actively addressed these, indicating a responsive approach that would be vital for any mobile port.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Combat Fluidity">
    Early feedback noted animation locks and clunky movement. Developers acknowledged this, promising fixes for later tests to improve the overall feel of combat. This is critical for both PC and potential mobile experiences.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Basic Attack Mechanics">
    Players disliked the 2-second cooldown on basic attacks, especially for ranged characters, making kiting difficult. The team is considering changes to allow spammable basic attacks, improving combat flow.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Progression Pace">
    Initial ability unlocks were considered too slow (levels 5, 10, 15). Developers adjusted this to levels 4, 8, and 12, making early game progression feel more rewarding.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="NPC Body Blocking">
    The issue of NPCs and party members blocking player movement was identified. This concern has been acknowledged and is slated for a fix, which would greatly enhance navigation and combat positioning.
  </Step>
</Steps>
