<Section icon={Target} title="Strategic Playstyle and Team Composition">
Zeugladius thrives in situations where his defensive and supportive capabilities can be fully utilized. His ability to provide burst healing and damage reduction makes him an excellent choice for raids, dungeons, and PvP modes where coordinated team play is essential.

<Callout type="success" title="Synergy Focus">
Pair Zeugladius with melee damage dealers or other front-line characters to maximize the effectiveness of his AoE abilities and invulnerability buff.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Front-Line Positioning">
    Always aim to be in the thick of the fight, protecting your squishier teammates. Use **Divine Shield** to mitigate incoming damage, especially when engaging large groups of enemies or boss attacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Mastering Flash of Light">
    This ability is your go-to for negating predictable burst damage. In scenarios like dragon breath attacks (e.g., Kaladrax in beta), a perfectly timed **Flash of Light** can allow your entire team to continue dealing damage without needing to reposition. Consider the teleport orb for aggressive plays or quick repositioning.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Optimal Healing with Holy Light">
    While it's a burst heal, its vast area makes it incredibly forgiving. Use **Holy Light** when multiple allies are taking significant damage, even if they are spread out. This ensures widespread sustain and keeps your team in the fight longer.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Crowd Control and Disruption">
    Zeugladius also has abilities that can reduce enemy movement speed and stun, which are invaluable for controlling the battlefield. Use these to interrupt dangerous enemy casts or to peel for your allies.
  </Step>
</Steps>
