### Common Gameplay Feedback and Solutions

| Aspect         | Player Feedback (2026)                                | Impact on Heroes                                      | Strategic Adjustments                                   |
| :------------- | :---------------------------------------------------- | :---------------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Movement**   | Often described as delayed or unresponsive.           | Hinders kiting for ranged, makes dodging difficult.   | Prioritize movement speed buffs, utilize hero dashes.   |
| **Animation Lock** | Every ability, including basic attacks, locks animation. | Prevents simultaneous movement and attacking.         | Time abilities carefully, anticipate enemy attacks.     |
| **Basic Attack Cooldown** | 2-second cooldown on basic attacks.                   | Creates "dead time" in early combat.                  | Focus on ability rotations, use abilities on cooldown.  |
| **Body Blocking** | NPCs and party members can body block players.        | Traps players, hinders movement in tight spaces.      | Maintain spatial awareness, position carefully in groups. |
| **Targeting**  | Keyboard aiming can be slow; no aiming behind character. | Impacts AoE placement, especially for ranged heroes.  | Practice aiming, use AoE strategically when enemies are grouped. |
