<Section icon={Map} title="Locating Sally and Starting the Quest">
Your journey to obtaining a mount begins with Sally, a key NPC located strategically on the map. Finding her and initiating the quest requires a bit of exploration and character progression.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Find Sally's Stable">
    Sally is located east of the starter town's inn. Open your map and follow the main road east from the inn until you reach her stable.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Meet Prerequisites">
    Initially, Sally might be too busy to speak with you. You'll need to advance your main story quest and increase your power level (e.g., to 460) and adventure rank. Return to the inn to level up your adventure rank if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Accept the Quest">
    Once a quest marker appears over Sally's head, speak with her. She will inform you that her donkey is lost, initiating the "Lost Donkey" quest.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
The specific power and adventure rank requirements might change upon the game's official launch, but generally, progressing through early game content will prepare you.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Rabbit} title="Herding the Lost Donkey: A Test of Patience">
The first challenge in your mount acquisition quest is locating Sally's lost donkey and guiding it back to her stable. This task requires patience and a good understanding of the donkey's rather stubborn AI.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Locating the Donkey">
    - Open your map after accepting the quest.
    - A large blue quest circle will indicate the general area.
    - Follow the main road south from the stable, then head west near the bottom of the quest circle.
    - The donkey is usually found within this area.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Steering Mechanics">
    - You need to push the donkey from behind to steer it.
    - Don't let it wander too far, or you might have to restart.
    - Letting the donkey walk on its own in the correct direction often works best.
    - Be prepared for the donkey to occasionally bolt into the forest, requiring redirection.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="warning" title="Donkey's Temperament">
The donkey can be quite unpredictable. It will wander off if left unattended or if pushed too aggressively in the wrong direction. Maintain a consistent, gentle push from behind to guide it effectively.
</Callout>

Successfully herding the donkey back is a crucial step before you can participate in any **Drakantos races**.

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Taming Your Donkey: The Pebble and Rope Technique">
Once the donkey is safely back at the stable, the next step is to tame it. This involves a unique minigame where you use pebbles and a rope to subdue your new companion.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Approach the Stable">
    Guide the donkey past the main stable entrance to the pen located around the back. You'll hear a "ding" and your quest marker will update when it's in the right spot.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Toss the Pebble">
    Speak with Sally. She'll instruct you to toss a pebble to get the donkey to run across the pen. This is essential for the next step.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Use the Rope">
    Hold your rope button (default 'I' key) while using WASD to position your AoE (Area of Effect) ahead of the running donkey. The goal is to make the donkey run into the rope's AoE.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reel it In">
    Once caught, hold the 'O' key (default) to reel the donkey in. The rope can break, so if the donkey runs away, let go, wait for it to stop, and then hold 'O' again.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Timing is Key">
Mastering the timing of letting go and holding the 'O' key is critical to prevent the rope from breaking. Observe the donkey's movements carefully.
</Callout>

</Section>

With your donkey tamed, the final test is to race Sally. This race is surprisingly challenging and serves as your introduction to **Drakantos races**. Winning this race will officially grant you ownership of your mount.

</CardGrid>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Activating and Using Your Donkey Mount">
After successfully winning the race against Sally, you're just a few steps away from riding your new donkey. Activating and using your mount is a straightforward process.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Open Inventory">
    Press 'B' to open your inventory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate Mount License">
    Navigate to the three-star tab within your inventory. You will find your mount license there.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Activate License">
    Select the license and press 'Enter' to activate it.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Equip Mount">
    Press 'Q' or 'E' to cycle to the cosmetics tab. Click on the empty tile with a yellow dot on the right side. Select your donkey and press 'Enter'.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Mount/Dismount">
    Press 'F' to mount or dismount your donkey.
  </Step>
</Steps>
