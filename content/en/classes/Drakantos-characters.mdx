<Section icon={Settings} title="Customizing Your Hero Mechanics">

What truly sets **Drakantos characters** apart is the depth of customization available after you have picked your hero. You aren't locked into a single way of playing. Through the use of Orbs and Artifacts, you can fundamentally alter how a hero's abilities function, allowing for "sub-builds" that can surprise opponents in PvP or overcome specific boss mechanics in PvE.

**Key Customization Components:**
- **Orbs:** These items modify specific skill behaviors, such as adding elemental damage or reducing cooldowns.
- **Artifacts:** Powerful gear pieces that provide passive bonuses or unique triggers during combat.
- **Skins & Collectibles:** Visual customization including exclusive icons, titles, and over 30 unique emotes per hero.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire Orbs and Artifacts">
    Clear dynamic dungeons and defeat bosses to drop rare items. Higher difficulty missions yield more potent modification tools.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Modify Ability Slots">
    Access the hero customization menu to equip Orbs. Each hero has specific slots that can be tuned to change skill properties.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Test Synergies">
    Combine specific Artifacts with modified Orbs to create a build. For example, a "Life Leach" build might combine healing Orbs with attack-speed Artifacts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Refine for PvP or PvE">
    Save different loadouts. In PvP, focus on utility and mobility; in PvE, focus on sustained damage and survivability.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="The Skill Factor">
Remember that in PvP, your character's raw stats (HP, Attack Power) are equalized. Your customization choices with Orbs and Artifacts are about *how* your skills work, not just making them "bigger."
</Callout>

| Customization Type | Impact on Gameplay | Acquisition Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Orbs** | Modifies skill mechanics/effects | Dungeon drops / Market |
| **Artifacts** | Provides passive combat bonuses | Boss loot / Crafting |
| **Emotes** | Social interaction/Voice lines | Unlocks / Achievements |
| **Skins** | Purely visual hero upgrades | Events / Store / Collectibles |

</Section>

<Section icon={Map} title="Progression and Living World">

Leveling your hero is only the beginning. Drakantos features a living world with day and night cycles that directly affect the enemies you face. A hero that excels in the daytime might find the nocturnal versions of enemies significantly more challenging due to different mechanics and increased aggression.

| World Mechanic | Effect on Heroes | Reward Potential |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Day Cycle** | Standard enemy behavior/visibility | Consistent XP / Basic resources |
| **Night Cycle** | Harder enemies / New mechanics | Rare drops / Unique Artifacts |
| **Dynamic Dungeons** | Changing traps and objectives | High-tier loot / Orbs |
| **Housing** | Personal space / Social hub | Customization / Showcase |

The Guild System also plays a massive role in character progression. By joining a powerful guild, you gain access to collective goals and the chance to become the most dominant force on the continent of Eldras.
