<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics and Skill Customization">

Combat for a **Drakantos Illusionist** is a delicate balance of timing and positioning. Unlike modern action-RPGs that favor button-mashing, Drakantos leans into a deliberate, almost turn-based cadence within a real-time environment. This "old-school" feel means that every spell cast and movement command carries significant weight. Players must manage cooldowns effectively, as there is currently a lack of complex mana management, turning most encounters into a test of cooldown rotation.

The customization of your hero is handled through two primary systems: Orbs and Artifacts. These items allow you to tweak your abilities to suit either solo play or group dynamics.

| Item Type | Function | Usage Limitation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Orbs** | Modify existing skill properties | Permanent until swapped |
| **Artifacts** | Provide active utility/items | Limited charges per mission |
| **Equipment** | Increases base Power score | Requires smelting to upgrade |

<Section icon={Target} title="Progression and Power Leveling">

To advance as a **Drakantos Illusionist**, you must climb the ranks of the Adventurer's Guild. Your rank is directly tied to your "Power" score, which functions similarly to an item level or gear score. Progression is not just about level; it is about the quality and reinforcement of your equipment.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Complete Guild Quests">
    Start with Rank 1 and 2 missions to gather basic green and blue gear.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Smelting and Reinforcement">
    Visit the forge to smelt unwanted items and use the resources to reinforce your primary gear.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Artifact Acquisition">
    Collect artifacts to fill your utility slots, providing poison barrels or health recovery.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Rank Advancement">
    Once your Power reaches the required threshold, complete the rank-up trial to unlock harder dungeons.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Gear Rarity | Drop Source | Smelting Value |

| **Uncommon (Green)** | Rank 1+ Missions | Medium |
| **Rare (Blue)** | Rank 2+ Missions | High |

<Section icon={Shield} title="Navigating Dungeons and Hazard Management">

Dungeons in Drakantos are dynamic, meaning the set of enemies, traps, and objectives can change with every playthrough. For an Illusionist, awareness of the environment is as important as combat prowess. Traps like falling boulders, cave bats, and beehives are notorious for killing players in a single hit if they are triggered accidentally.

For those who have mastered the **Drakantos Illusionist** in PvE, the PvP system offers a balanced battleground. Inspired by competitive titles like League of Legends and Brawl Stars, Drakantos PvP normalizes stats so that only skill and strategy matter.
