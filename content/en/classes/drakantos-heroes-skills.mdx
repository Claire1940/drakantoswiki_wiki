<Section icon={Map} title="Combat Mechanics and Skill Interaction">
The transition to a full action combat system significantly impacts how **Drakantos heroes skills** are utilized. Players now have free movement and must aim their attacks and abilities with the mouse.

| Combat Mechanic      | Description                                                                     | Impact on Skills                                                              |
| :------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | :---------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Free Movement**    | No longer tile-based; full 360° movement.                                       | Allows for better positioning and dodging during skill usage.                 |
| **Action Combat**    | No target system; skills must be aimed with the mouse.                          | Requires precision and spatial awareness for effective skill hits.            |
| **Basic Attacks**    | Left-click for basic, right-click for special (hold for enhanced).              | Basic attacks have a stamina-like system, timing is crucial for optimal DPS. |
| **Controller Support**| Right analog stick for turning direction; fluid 360° movement.                  | Offers an alternative control scheme, potentially smoother for dashes.        |

Certain skills and artifacts may have effects that state "up to X seconds." This usually implies that the effect duration is reduced against players or bosses for balancing purposes, ensuring that powerful **Drakantos heroes skills** aren't overly dominant in high-stakes encounters.

<Callout type="info">
Your character automatically faces the mouse direction when attacking, which streamlines the action combat experience and helps maintain focus on aiming your **Drakantos heroes skills**.
</Callout>
