<Section icon={Swords} title="Core Ability Breakdown">

Thomas's kit is designed around the concept of "Strong Tunes" and "Musical Wards." Understanding the nuances of each ability is critical for maximizing your contribution to a party. The **drakantos bard** relies on precise timing and positioning to keep allies alive while disrupting enemy formations.

| Ability Name | Primary Effect | Key Utility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic Attack** | Single target magic damage | Fast cast speed with orbs |
| **Slide** | Forward dash with 5% DR | Shielding and speed buffs |
| **Strong Tune** | Shoots 3 magical notes | Precision hits deal extra damage |
| **Musical Ward** | Buffs ally with DR/AOE damage | Includes a pushback reactivation |
| **Resonance** | 4-chord rhythm sequence | AOE Sleep and team shielding |
| **Song of Healing** | 8s AOE healing aura | Proximity-based mobile sustain |

<Callout type="tip" title="Combat Strategy">
Always use **Musical Ward** on your frontline tank. Not only does it provide damage reduction, but the pulsing AOE damage helps maintain aggro and clear smaller mobs.
</Callout>

The **Slide** ability is Thomas's primary survival tool. Unlike standard dashes, it provides a built-in damage reduction (DR) of 5%, which can be enhanced through Orbs to provide a massive 15% max health shield. This makes Thomas surprisingly durable for a caster-class hero.

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering the Resonance Mechanic">

The defining feature of the **drakantos bard** is the **Resonance** ability. This skill introduces a mini-game similar to the "Dancer" steps in other popular MMOs. To successfully cast Resonance, you must input a sequence of four chords displayed on the screen.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Activation">
    Trigger the Resonance skill to bring up the chord sequence UI. The game will display four specific colored notes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Chord Input">
    Press the corresponding buttons in the exact order shown. Speed is essential here, as the cooldown scales with your performance.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Sleep Trigger">
    Upon a perfect completion, Thomas deals AOE damage and puts all nearby enemies to sleep, providing vital breathing room.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Shield Accumulation">
    If the final Orb is equipped, every successful step in the sequence adds a layer of shielding to all nearby allies.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Input Error">
Missing a chord usually results in an error, cancelling the effect. However, the first Orb upgrade allows for one mistake, which is highly recommended for beginners learning the rhythm.
</Callout>

Resonance is particularly effective in **Escort Missions**. When new waves of mobs spawn, a well-timed Resonance can put the entire pack to sleep, allowing the escort target to pass safely while the party focuses on priority targets.

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Orb Customization and Progression">

Orbs in Drakantos allow you to specialize your hero's abilities. For Thomas, Orbs transform him from a simple healer into a utility powerhouse. Proper Orb selection can significantly reduce the "gimmicky" feel of his rhythm mechanics by adding safety nets and cooldown reductions.

| Skill | Orb 1 Effect | Orb 2 Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Slide** | Extra charges / Adjusted CD | Increased distance and DR duration |
| **Basic Attack** | +15% Cast speed | +25% Cast speed |
| **Strong Tune** | Center-hit bonus notes | Faster manual aiming speed |
| **Musical Ward** | Enemy movement reduction | Pushback on reactivation |
| **Resonance** | One-miss forgiveness | CD reduction based on speed |

<Callout type="success" title="High-Level Build">
For endgame content, prioritize the **Resonance Orb 2**. The cooldown reduction allows you to chain AOE sleeps, which is arguably the strongest CC in the current Drakantos meta.
</Callout>

The final Orb for Resonance also adds a shield of 15% max health to allies. When combined with the Song of Healing, Thomas can effectively "nullify" incoming burst damage while simultaneously topping off the party's health bars.

</Section>
