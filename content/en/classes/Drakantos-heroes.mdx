<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Customizing Hero Abilities with Orbs and Artifacts">

This system allows players to tailor their hero's performance to their preferred playstyle or to counter specific opponents in PvP. This deep customization ensures that even with a fixed set of heroes, the strategic possibilities remain vast.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Acquire Beta Boxes">
    During the Closed Beta, players obtained special Beta Boxes through PvP and PvE activities. These boxes contained Orbs, Artifacts, and Trophies, crucial for testing different builds. While these specific boxes are beta-exclusive, the full game will offer similar progression methods.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Open Orb Boxes Strategically">
    When opening an Orb Box, it's crucial to be playing the hero you intend to receive an Orb for. One of the three options provided in the box is guaranteed to be specifically for your current hero, ensuring you get relevant upgrades.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Equip and Experiment with Artifacts">
    Artifacts provide powerful effects that can drastically change a hero's playstyle. Experiment with different Artifact combinations to find synergistic builds. Remember that some skill and artifact effects may be reduced against players or bosses for balance reasons.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Modify Abilities">
    Orbs allow direct modification of a hero's existing abilities, potentially adding new effects, increasing damage, or reducing cooldowns. This is where you can truly fine-tune your hero's kit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="PvP Impact on Hero Selection">
Drakantos places a strong emphasis on PvP combat, offering various modes where hero choice and customization are paramount. The game allows players to progress and level up through PvP, making hero mastery essential for competitive success. During PvP matches, all heroes are set to max level with maxed equipment and skills, meaning the strategic advantage comes from understanding hero kits, build choices (Orbs, Artifacts, Trophies), and player skill.

| PvP Mode       | Objective                                        | Hero Considerations                                 | Reward Scaling         |
| :------------- | :----------------------------------------------- | :-------------------------------------------------- | :--------------------- |
| **Control**    | Stand on and capture control zones.              | Area denial, mobility, sustain for zone holding.    | Standard               |
| **Escort**     | Escort your crystal, prevent enemy escort.       | Crowd control, burst damage to stop enemy push, protection for crystal carrier. | Standard               |
| **Assault**    | Destroy enemy towers and core (MOBA-like).       | Siege potential, wave clear, strong dueling heroes. | Higher (due to match length) |

<Callout type="success" title="PvP Balance">
In PvP, artifacts and trophies are maximized, but their effects can be balanced. For instance, artifact charges are halved in Control and Escort modes, and some "up to X seconds" effects are reduced against players.
</Callout>
