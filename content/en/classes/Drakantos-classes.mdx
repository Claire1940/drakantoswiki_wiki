### The Customization Process: Steps to Craft Your Build

Building your ideal hero in Drakantos involves several layers of customization.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Hero">
    Begin by choosing one of the unique heroes. This initial choice defines your hero's core abilities and thematic focus. Remember, you can swap heroes in town, so feel free to experiment.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip Gear & Level Abilities">
    As you play, you'll acquire equipment that enhances your hero's stats. Simultaneously, your hero's base abilities will level up, increasing their potency.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Integrate Orbs">
    Orbs are crucial modifiers for your hero's abilities. You can slot these into your skills to change their effects. For example, a basic attack might be transformed into a flaming sword attack by applying a specific Orb. These Orbs can often be traded between players, fostering a dynamic in-game economy.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Utilize Artifacts">
    Artifacts provide significant buffs and unique effects that can drastically alter your hero's performance. They are powerful items that complement your chosen Orbs and playstyle. During beta, special Beta Boxes allowed players to test various endgame Artifacts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Collect Trophies">
    Trophies are passive items that grant your hero various bonuses. These can range from stat increases to unique passive effects that further refine your build and synergize with your active abilities and artifacts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Example of Customization Impact

Consider the hero Iris, known for her arcane power.
- **Base Ability**: Arcane Blast (a standard ranged attack).
- **With Orb**: An Orb might transform Arcane Blast into "Explosive Arcane Shard," causing it to deal area-of-effect damage upon impact.
- **With Artifact**: An Artifact could grant "Echoing Power," causing every third Arcane ability to cast twice.
- **With Trophy**: A Trophy might provide "Mana Flow," increasing spell damage based on current mana percentage.

Combining these elements allows for highly specialized builds, turning a simple ranged caster into a devastating AoE mage with burst potential.
