<Section icon={Shield} title="Action Combat and Movement Mechanics">

The transition from a hybrid system to a 100% action combat system has fundamentally changed how the **drakantos berserker** functions in both PvE and PvP. Every basic attack and special ability must be aimed manually. Left-clicking performs your standard combo, while holding the right-click button activates an enhanced special attack. For the Berserker, this often results in a massive AoE blast or a powerful forward lunge that can decimate groups of smaller NPCs.

| Mechanic | Keyboard & Mouse | Controller Support |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Movement** | 8-directional movement | Full 360-degree fluid movement |
| **Targeting** | Aimed via mouse cursor | Aimed via right analog stick |
| **Attacking** | Left click (Basic) / Right click (Special) | Trigger-based inputs |
| **Precision** | High precision for skill shots | Higher mobility and comfort |
| **Complexity** | Easy to learn for PC veterans | Requires time to master 360 aiming |

<Callout type="warning" title="Important">
The targeting system can feel "sticky" in the current beta build. If your character is not facing the correct way, skills may not fire in the direction of the mouse. Always ensure your character's model is oriented toward the threat before committing to a long charge animation.
</Callout>

Movement is no longer restricted to a grid. You can dash and dodge in any direction, which is vital for a melee class that needs to avoid telegraphed boss attacks. Melee attacks in Drakantos have a "stamina-like" quality; while you can use them outside of cooldowns, timing your strikes every two seconds remains the most optimal way to maintain high DPS without exhausting your character's combat flow.

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Build Optimization: Artifacts and Orbs">

Progression for the **drakantos berserker** involves more than just leveling up. The game features a deep system of Orbs, Artifacts, and Trophies that modify your abilities. During the beta, players receive special Beta Boxes to help them test high-end builds that would normally take weeks to acquire.

| Item Type | Function | Guarantee Mechanic |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Orb Box** | Grants hero-specific ability modifiers | 1 of 3 options is for your current hero |
| **Artifact Box** | Provides passive combat bonuses | Randomized selection of 3 artifacts |
| **Trophy Box** | Offers endgame stat boosts | Randomized selection of 3 trophies |
| **Skill Orbs** | Changes skill behavior (e.g., Pyro Fire Orb) | Must be applied via the character menu |

<Callout type="success" title="Build Tip">
When opening an Orb Box, always ensure you are currently playing as the hero you want the orb for. The game guarantees that at least one of the three choices will be compatible with your active character.
</Callout>

For a Berserker build, look for artifacts that provide health regeneration or damage reduction. The "Water Skin" artifact, for instance, can provide 40 health per second for 10 seconds after a battle, significantly reducing downtime between pulls. You can also find skill modifiers like the Pyro gather fire orb, which deals 80 damage in a small area, perfect for clearing out spider infestations or groups of NPCs in dungeons.

</Section>
