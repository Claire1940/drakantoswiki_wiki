<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Optimizing Your Drakantos Experience">
Beyond class choice, several factors can significantly enhance your Drakantos gameplay, from connection stability to build optimization. A smooth and responsive experience is paramount in an action MMORPG.

### Connection Stability for Competitive Play

Fast-paced combat demands a stable connection. Lag spikes can disrupt skill rotations, delay crucial dodges, or interrupt abilities, especially during intense PvP or boss fights. Tools like ExitLag can help by reducing ping, stabilizing connections, and preventing packet loss, leading to a much smoother and more responsive gaming experience.

### Build Optimization: Beyond the Basics

Finding the right build is as important as choosing the right hero. Even a top-tier hero can underperform without proper skill combinations and gear optimization. Builds should focus on synergy between abilities, mobility, and survivability, tailored to your chosen hero and content.

<Callout type="success" title="Synergy is Key">
Always prioritize synergy within your build. A combination of abilities and gear that complement each other will almost always outperform a collection of individually strong pieces that don't work together.
</Callout>
