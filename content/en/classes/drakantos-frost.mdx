<Section icon={Shield} title="Drakantos Frost Strategies in PvP and PvE">
The versatility of **Drakantos Frost** abilities shines in both player-versus-player (PvP) and player-versus-environment (PvE) encounters. Adapting your strategy to the specific challenges of each mode will ensure your success.

### PvP Tactics

In PvP, frost abilities are invaluable for controlling the battlefield and isolating key targets.

| PvP Scenario | Frost Strategy | Key Benefits |
|:-------------|:---------------|:-------------|
| **Team Fights** | Focus crowd control on high-priority enemy DPS or healers. Use AoE freezes to lock down multiple opponents. | Disrupts enemy formations, creates opportunities for allies, prevents escape. |
| **Duels** | Utilize single-target freezes and slows to kite opponents and maintain distance. Burst damage with ice spells when the enemy is vulnerable. | Controls engagement range, allows for sustained damage, reduces incoming damage. |
| **Objective Control** | Use frost abilities to slow enemies attempting to capture objectives, giving your team time to react. | Denies objective progress, forces enemy repositioning, buys valuable time. |

### PvE Dungeon Runs

In PvE, frost is excellent for managing large groups of enemies and neutralizing dangerous bosses.

| PvE Scenario | Frost Strategy | Key Benefits |
|:-------------|:---------------|:-------------|
| **Mob Clearing** | Group enemies together and use AoE frost spells to freeze or slow them, then unleash follow-up damage. | Reduces incoming damage, makes clearing faster, safer engagement. |
| **Boss Encounters** | Use single-target freezes on adds or during boss vulnerability phases. Apply slows to reduce boss attack speed or movement. | Mitigates boss mechanics, creates damage windows, enhances team survivability. |
| **Escort Missions** | Slow or freeze enemies that target the escort objective, protecting it from damage. | Ensures mission success, reduces stress on healers, maintains objective health. |
