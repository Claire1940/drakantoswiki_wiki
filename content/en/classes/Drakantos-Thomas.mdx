**Drakantos Thomas** possesses a distinctive set of abilities that allow him to control the battlefield, support allies, and even contribute to damage. Each ability can be enhanced with Orbs, significantly altering their effectiveness and utility.

**Thomas's Ability Overview**

| Ability Name | Type | Description | Orb Upgrades (Key Benefits) |

<Callout type="warning" title="Resonance Mastery">

</Callout>

His **Musical Ward** is excellent for protecting a key ally while also applying pressure to surrounding foes. The ability to reactivate it for a push-back effect provides crucial disengage potential.
