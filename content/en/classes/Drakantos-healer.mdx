<Section icon={Trophy} title="Strategic Play for Drakantos Healers">
Effective healing in Drakantos goes beyond simply pressing healing buttons. It involves understanding encounter mechanics, anticipating damage, and coordinating with your team.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Anticipate Damage">
    Learn boss patterns and enemy attack timings to pre-cast shields (Marly's Divine Protection) or position AoE heals (Marly's Seriel's Light) before heavy damage occurs. Byron players should anticipate when a tank will take critical damage to time Time Rejuvenation effectively.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Prioritize Targets">
    Identify who needs healing most. Often, this is the tank, but DPS or even yourself might require immediate attention. Marly's ability to shield any ally is crucial here. Byron's single-target focus means he must be decisive.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Utilize Crowd Control">
    A good Drakantos healer doesn't just heal; they control the flow of battle. Marly's Chastise and Holy Nova can root and slow enemies, providing breathing room. Byron's Meteor, Hurricane, and Blizzard offer powerful stun, pushback, and freeze effects.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Master Positioning">
    Healers are often squishy. Marly needs to be close enough for her AoE heals but safe from direct attacks. Byron's Time Warp is an excellent escape tool, allowing him to quickly reposition or avoid danger.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Communicate with Your Team">
    Inform your team about cooldowns, especially for critical abilities like invulnerability shields or major heals. Coordinate burst damage phases with your team's defensive cooldowns.
  </Step>
</Steps>
