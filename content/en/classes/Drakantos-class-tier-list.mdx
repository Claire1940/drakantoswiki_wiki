<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Customizing Your Hero: Orbs and Artifacts">

The most unique feature of Drakantos is that the **Drakantos class tier list** is not static. Every one of the 21 heroes can be modified. This system is what allows a B-Tier hero like **Thomas** or **Liam** to compete with S-Tier legends. 

Customization happens through two primary items:
1.  **Orbs**: These modify base stats and can sometimes add secondary effects to your active abilities.
2.  **Artifacts**: These are rarer items that can fundamentally change how a hero's mechanic works (e.g., turning a single-target heal into an AOE pulse).

| Item Type | Customization Depth | Acquisition Method | Impact on Tier |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Common Orbs** | Basic Stat Boosts | Standard Dungeons | Low |
| **Epic Orbs** | Skill Modifiers | Hard Mode Missions | Medium |
| **Legendary Artifacts** | Mechanical Overhauls | Weekly Tournaments | High |
| **Unique Artifacts** | Hero-Specific Changes | Special Events | Very High |

<Callout type="info" title="System Note">
The Day and Night cycle in Eldras can actually activate hidden properties in certain artifacts. For example, some artifacts might only provide their "S-Tier" buffs during the night.
</Callout>
