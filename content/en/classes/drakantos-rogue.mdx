<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering Ozul's Combat Flow">
The Drakantos Rogue excels through a dynamic combat style that weaves movement, stealth, and precise attacks. Understanding how Ozul's abilities synergize is key to unlocking his full potential.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate with Shadowstep">
    Use **Shadowstep** to quickly close distances, reposition, or evade. Its initial damage reduction is valuable, and upgrading it for extra charges or distance provides greater tactical flexibility. Perfect timing with orbs can grant attack and movement speed buffs, further enhancing your opener.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Exploit Positioning with Sinister Strike">
    After repositioning, aim to hit enemies with **Sinister Strike** from behind. This grants a substantial 40% damage increase. Holding the ability for 1.2 seconds maximizes its damage, and certain orb upgrades allow Ozul to automatically Shadowstep behind an enemy for this attack.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Maintain Pressure with Shadow Dance and Phantom Daggers">
    **Shadow Dance** is a powerful AoE ability that also resets **Shadowstep's** cooldown if it hits at least one enemy. This creates a loop of mobility and damage. Follow up with **Phantom Daggers** to convert your basic attacks into ranged throws, increasing your attack range. The daggers left on the ground from Shadow Dance (with orb upgrade) or Phantom Daggers can be reactivated for additional damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Utilize Vanish for Burst and Escape">
    **Vanish** is your ultimate tool for stealth and burst damage. Become invisible for up to 5 seconds, gaining a 40% movement speed bonus. While invisible, approaching an enemy and canceling Vanish with either **Sinister Strike** or **Shadow Dance** will grant those attacks additional damage. This is perfect for initiating fights, escaping danger, or re-engaging from a favorable position.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Stealth Limitations">
While **Vanish** makes you untargetable, it does not make you immune to AoE abilities. Being hit by an AoE will cancel your stealth, so be mindful of enemy attacks even when invisible.
</Callout>

</Section>
