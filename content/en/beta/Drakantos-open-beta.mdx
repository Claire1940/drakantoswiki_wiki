<Section icon={Map} title="Major Gameplay Changes and Features">
The **Drakantos Open Beta** brings forth a host of major gameplay changes designed to enhance the player experience. The most notable shifts include the move from tile-based movement to fully free, 360-degree movement, and a complete overhaul to a full action combat system. This means skills must now be aimed with the mouse, making combat more dynamic and engaging.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Movement System">
    - **Previous Beta**: Tile-based, limited movement.
    - **Open Beta**: Fully free, 360° movement, including dashes.
    - **Impact**: Smoother navigation and more fluid combat.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Combat System">
    - **Previous Beta**: Hybrid combat with target system.
    - **Open Beta**: Full action combat, no target system.
    - **Impact**: Skills require aiming, increased player agency.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

These changes mean that even familiar heroes may feel significantly different, offering a fresh experience. For those less interested in PvP, the PvE content from the previous beta remains available, providing a stable environment to test these new core mechanics.

<Callout type="warning" title="Progress Wipe Notice">
Please be aware that all progress made during the **Drakantos Open Beta** will be **wiped** before the game's full release. This is standard practice for beta tests to ensure a fair and balanced launch.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Drakantos PvP Modes Explained">
A significant addition to the **Drakantos Open Beta** is the introduction of PvP, featuring 13 maps across three distinct modes. These modes are designed to offer varied tactical experiences and reward competitive play. Players will have access to special Beta Boxes through both PvP and PvE activities, which grant endgame Trophies, Orbs, and Artifacts to test different builds.

Here's a breakdown of the PvP modes:

| PvP Mode | Objective                                            | Key Mechanics                                         | Rewards                                             |
|:---------|:-----------------------------------------------------|:------------------------------------------------------|:----------------------------------------------------|
| **Control** | Stand on control zones to earn progress.             | Capture and hold zones; first team to fully capture wins. | Faster achievements due to shorter matches.         |
| **Escort** | Escort your crystal to the final point.              | Crystal moves with allies, stops near enemies.        | Faster achievements due to shorter matches.         |
| **Assault** | Destroy enemy towers and core.                       | MOBA-like with shared tower health, minion priority.  | More rewards due to longer match duration.          |

<Callout type="info" title="PvP Equipping & Scaling">
In PvP, all players are set to max level with maxed equipment and skills. Trophies and Artifacts are also maximized, allowing you to focus purely on strategy and build synergy without the grind.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Twitch Drops and Rewards">
Participating in the **Drakantos Open Beta** can be even more rewarding through Twitch Drops. Players can earn exclusive in-game items by simply watching any channel streaming Drakantos in its designated category on Twitch. This is a great way to acquire temporary beta-exclusive items that can further enhance your testing experience.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Download Drakantos">
    Ensure you have the Drakantos client installed and ready to play.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Link Twitch Account">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Watch Drakantos Streams">

  <Step num="4" title="Claim Rewards">

  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Temporary Rewards">
