<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Major Gameplay Improvements and Combat Overhaul">

The Drakantos beta test introduces a complete revamp of its core gameplay mechanics, significantly enhancing the combat and movement experience. The most notable change is the transition from a tile-based grid system to fully freeform movement, allowing for more dynamic and intuitive character control. This is coupled with updated mouse controls and a shift to a full action combat system, moving away from the previous hybrid model.

<Callout type="tip" title="Mastering New Controls">
The updated control scheme, especially for mouse and keyboard, is designed for free-aiming attacks. Left-click for basic attacks, right-click for special attacks. For controllers, the right analog stick controls attack direction, similar to a twin-stick shooter. Practice these new inputs to maximize your combat effectiveness.
</Callout>

These changes mean that heroes will feel different to play, even if you were familiar with them in the first beta. Players are encouraged to re-familiarize themselves with their chosen characters to adapt to the new flow of combat. The developers have also addressed basic attacks, allowing them to be used outside of cooldowns but with a stamina-like system, ensuring strategic engagement remains key.

**Key Gameplay Changes:**

| Feature | Previous Beta (Tile-based) | Current Beta (Freeform) | Impact |
| :------ | :------------------------- | :---------------------- | :----- |
| **Movement** | Grid-based, 8 directions | Fully free, 360-degree | More fluid, precise positioning. |
| **Combat** | Hybrid target system | Full action combat, free aim | More engaging, skill-shot oriented. |
| **Mouse Control** | Limited targeting | Revamped for direct aiming | Intuitive attack direction. |
| **Basic Attacks** | Cooldown dependent | Stamina-like system | Allows continuous engagement with strategic pacing. |
| **Dashes** | Directionally limited | 360-degree aiming | Enhanced mobility and evasive maneuvers. |

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Drakantos PvP Modes and Beta Boxes">

The primary focus of this Drakantos beta test is to thoroughly test the new PvP systems, featuring 13 maps across three distinct game modes: Control, Escort, and Assault. These modes offer varied objectives and strategic depth, catering to different playstyles. To facilitate comprehensive testing of builds and endgame content, players will receive special Beta Boxes. These temporary boxes grant access to a wide variety of Orbs, Artifacts, and Trophies, which are crucial for shaping character builds.

<Callout type="warning" title="Beta Box Usage Reminder">
When opening an Orb Box, ensure you are using the hero you intend to receive the Orb for. One of the options is guaranteed to be for your current hero, so selecting the correct character is vital to optimize your build.
</Callout>

### PvP Modes Breakdown

| Mode     | Objective                                                | Key Mechanics                                                     | Rewards & Achievements                                         |
| :------- | :------------------------------------------------------- | :---------------------------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Control** | Capture and hold control zones.                          | Stand on zones to earn progress; maps can have 1 or 2 points.     | Faster for achievement completion due to shorter matches.      |
| **Escort** | Escort your crystal to its destination, prevent enemy escort. | Crystal moves with allies, stops near enemies; track progress on minimap. | Faster for achievement completion due to shorter matches.      |
| **Assault** | Destroy enemy towers and then their core.                | MOBA-like with towers, cores, minions; buffs appear mid-map.      | More rewards due to longer match duration.                     |

In PvP modes, all characters are set to max level with maxed equipment and skills, ensuring a fair playing field focused purely on player skill and build choices. Trophies and Artifacts are also maximized to allow for diverse build experimentation.

| **Artifact Box** | Various Artifacts                            | Experiment with different artifact sets and their synergies.   |
| **Trophy Box** | Various Trophies                             | Unlocks endgame trophies to explore advanced playstyles.       |

These beta boxes are exclusive to the test period and will not carry over to the final game. They are designed solely to provide players with a wide range of options to test and provide feedback on the game's balance and mechanics.

<Section icon={Map} title="Server Regions, Localization, and Technical Details">

To accommodate a global player base, the Drakantos beta test will feature four distinct server regions: North America (NA), South America (SA), Europe (EU), and Asia (AS). Players must choose their region carefully, as it cannot be changed during the beta. This ensures optimal latency and a stable connection for participants worldwide.
