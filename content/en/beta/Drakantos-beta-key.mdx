The most direct way to get a beta key is by requesting access through the Drakantos Steam page. This process is straightforward and allows any interested player to put their name in the hat for an invite.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Visit the Steam Store Page">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Click 'Request Access'">
    Click this button to submit your application for the Closed Beta. Steam will register your interest, and you will be considered for an invite.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Check Your Email for Invites">
    If selected, you will receive an email from Steam notifying you of your access. The first wave of participants will be drawn on **July 25, 2026**. More invites will be sent throughout the beta period.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Utilize Friend Invites">
    Players who get in will receive a friend invite that can be passed on to one other person. Ensure you've been Steam friends for at least 30 days to send the invite, as per Steam rules.
