### What Are Beta Boxes?
Beta Boxes are special containers that grant access to powerful in-game items. They are crucial for players to explore various build possibilities and understand the depth of Drakantos's customization system.

| Box Type      | Contents                                     | Purpose                                     | Rarity            |
|---------------|----------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------|-------------------|
| **Orb Box**   | Hero-specific Orbs, new modifiers            | Enhance hero abilities, alter playstyle     | Common, Rare, Epic|
| **Artifact Box**| Unique active abilities, stat boosts         | Provide tactical advantages in combat       | Common, Rare, Epic|
| **Trophy Box**| Endgame passive buffs, cosmetic items        | Boost overall power, customize appearance   | Common, Rare, Epic|

<Section icon={Swords} title="Earning Rewards Through Gameplay">
Drakantos offers multiple avenues to earn these valuable beta rewards, primarily through engaging with its core gameplay loops: Player-versus-Player (PvP) and Player-versus-Environment (PvE) content.

### PvP Activities
The Closed Beta introduces PvP for the first time, featuring 13 maps across three distinct modes. Engaging in these modes is an excellent way to earn Beta Boxes.

| PvP Mode   | Objective                                     | Rewards Focus                      | Achievements Speed |
|------------|-----------------------------------------------|------------------------------------|--------------------|
| **Control**| Stand on control zones, earn progress         | Balanced rewards                   | Faster             |
| **Escort** | Escort your crystal, prevent enemy escort     | Balanced rewards                   | Faster             |
| **Assault**| Destroy enemy towers and core                 | More rewards (longer matches)      | Slower             |

<Callout type="info" title="PvP Specifics">
In Control and Escort modes, artifact charges are reduced by half. Assault mode offers more rewards due to longer match durations, but achievements are typically faster to complete in Control/Escort.
</Callout>

### PvE Adventures
While the beta's primary focus is on PvP and new gameplay mechanics, the PvE content from previous beta tests remains available. This allows players to test new movement and control schemes while still earning rewards.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access Missions">
    Open the main menu and navigate to the "Adventure" section. Here, you'll find unlocked missions suitable for your current level.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select a Mission">
    Use Q and E keys to navigate through available missions. Each mission displays its recommended level and potential rewards.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Join or Start">
    You can join ongoing missions with other players or start a new one. Be aware that enemy strength scales with the number of players.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Complete Objectives">
    Fulfill the mission objectives, which typically involve killing mobs or looting specific items. Upon completion, you'll receive XP, loot, and resources, including Beta Boxes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Early Game Progression Tip">

<Section icon={Shield} title="Claiming and Utilizing Your Rewards">
Once you've earned your **Drakantos Beta Rewards**, understanding how to claim and effectively use them is key to exploring different builds.

### Opening Beta Boxes
Each Beta Box type (Orb, Artifact, Trophy) allows you to choose one item from three different options.

| Reward Type | Key Consideration                                        |
|-------------|----------------------------------------------------------|
| **Orb Box** | **Crucial:** Open with the hero you intend to get an Orb for, as one option is guaranteed for your current hero. |
| **Artifact Box**| Provides unique active abilities; experiment with different combinations to find synergistic builds. |
| **Trophy Box**| Offers powerful passive buffs; choose based on your desired endgame strategy and hero playstyle. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Orb Selection Strategy">
Always ensure you are playing the **correct hero** when opening an Orb Box. An Orb chosen for a different hero will not be as effective or useful for your current main.
</Callout>

### Upgrading Abilities and Gear
The items obtained from Beta Boxes can be used to upgrade your heroes.

| Item Type | Usage                                      | Location/NPC                     |
|-----------|--------------------------------------------|----------------------------------|
| **Orbs**  | Enhance specific hero abilities            | Ability menu (Press 'B')         |
| **Artifacts**| Equip for unique active skills             | Inventory/Character screen       |
| **Trophies**| Equip for passive buffs, stat increases    | Inventory/Character screen       |
| **Credits**| Purchase items, possibly upgrade materials | Market, various vendors          |

<Callout type="tip" title="Experiment with Builds">
The beta is the perfect time to experiment freely. Don't hesitate to try out different combinations of Orbs, Artifacts, and Trophies to discover powerful and fun builds for your favorite heroes.
</Callout>

### Twitch Drops
Drakantos also offers rewards through Twitch Drops. By watching Drakantos streams on Twitch, you can earn additional temporary items.
