<Section icon={Swords} title="Gameplay Reworks and Combat Enhancements">
The **Drakantos next beta** introduces substantial reworks to the game's core combat and movement systems, aiming for a more fluid and responsive action combat experience. This is a direct response to feedback from the first beta, which featured a tile-based grid system.

<YouTubeEmbed videoId="1Gf9GJ_mCIw" title="EVERYTHING We Know about Drakantos in 2026! New Beta Keys!" />

**Video Highlights:**
- The entire movement and combat system has been reworked for the new beta.
- Transition from stiff tile-based movement to freeform, 360-degree movement.
- Combat is now fully action-based with mouse aiming for attacks.

The most notable change is the shift from a tile-based movement system to a **fully freeform 360-degree movement**. This allows for more precise positioning and dynamic combat. Coupled with this, the combat system has evolved into a full action-based model, moving away from a hybrid or target-based approach.

<Callout type="success" title="New Combat Mechanics">
Players can now enjoy fully free movement, updated mouse controls, and a shift to full action combat. This means no more clicking enemies to attack; instead, you aim with your mouse for directional attacks and skills.
</Callout>

### Control Scheme Overview

| Action          | Keyboard & Mouse      | Controller (Xbox/PlayStation) | Notes                                           |
|:----------------|:----------------------|:------------------------------|:------------------------------------------------|
| **Movement**    | WASD                  | Left Analog Stick             | Now 360-degree, more fluid                    |
| **Aiming**      | Mouse                 | Right Analog Stick            | Essential for directional attacks & skills      |
| **Basic Attack**| Left Click            | (Controller specific mapping) | Stamina-based system, 2-second interval for max damage |
| **Special Attack**| Right Click           | (Controller specific mapping) |                                                 |
| **Ping Wheel**  | Mouse Wheel Button    | (Controller specific mapping) | For quick communication                         |
| **Scoreboard**  | Tab                   | (Controller specific mapping) |                                                 |
| **Bug Report**  | Ctrl + R              | (Not applicable)              | Be specific in your bug descriptions            |

Melee attacks have increased tolerance due to their charge mechanics, balancing the new freeform combat. While keyboard and mouse offer easier aiming, controller movement is designed to be more fluid, though it may require some adjustment.

</Section>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="PvP Modes and Beta Boxes">
The **Drakantos next beta** is primarily a PvP-focused experience, introducing three distinct modes to test the new combat system. To facilitate diverse build testing, players will receive special "beta boxes" filled with temporary in-game items.

### New PvP Modes

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Control">
    - **Objective:** Maintain control over a designated zone.
    - **Gameplay:** Stay within the circle longer than opponents.
    - **Rewards:** Faster achievements, shorter matches.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Escort">
    - **Objective:** Protect a crystal as it moves along a set path.
    - **Gameplay:** Stay close to the crystal to ensure its progression.
    - **Rewards:** Faster achievements, shorter matches.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Assault (MOBA-like)">
    - **Objective:** Destroy enemy towers and cores.
    - **Gameplay:** Kill minions, level up, push lanes, destroy structures.
    - **Rewards:** Higher rewards proportional to longer playtime.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="info">
In PvP modes, all characters are set to max level with max equipment and skills. Trophies and artifacts are also maximized to ensure a fair and balanced playing field, focusing purely on player skill and build synergy.
</Callout>

### Beta Boxes and Build Testing

To encourage experimentation with the new combat system and hero abilities, the beta will provide players with temporary "beta boxes." These boxes contain orbs, artifacts, and other in-game items designed for build crafting.

| Box Type      | Contents                                     | Purpose                                            |
|:--------------|:---------------------------------------------|:---------------------------------------------------|
| **Orb Box**   | Various Orbs                                 | Modify hero abilities, create unique skill effects |
| **Artifact Box**| Diverse Artifacts                            | Enhance passive abilities, alter gameplay          |
| **Trophy Box**| Trophies                                     | Grant powerful passive bonuses                     |

**Important Note:** When opening Orb Boxes, ensure you are on the hero you intend to upgrade, as the items are specific to the active character. These beta box items are temporary and will not carry over to the final game.

### PvE Experience

The PvE content in this beta will be identical to the first. This decision was made to avoid spoiling story progression and new PvE elements that are still under development. Players can still:

- Encounter a tuned-down endgame boss, usually found at Rank 8+, providing a taste of later challenges.
