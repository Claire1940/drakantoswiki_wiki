### How to Join the Beta

Access to the Drakantos closed beta is managed through Steam. Here’s a breakdown of how you can participate:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Previous Participants">
    If you received access during the prior beta, your access remains active. You do not need to re-request it.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="New Players">
    New players can request access directly through the official Drakantos Steam page. Invitations will be sent out gradually over time.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Friend Invites">
    Players who already have access can invite up to three friends, provided they have been Steam friends for at least 30 days. This is a Steam platform rule, not a Drakantos specific one.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Server Regions">
    Four server regions are available: North America (NA), South America (SA), Europe (EU), and Asia (AS). Once a region is selected, it cannot be changed during this beta test.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Server Stability Notice">
Due to major gameplay changes, servers are under significantly heavier load. A queue system may be implemented to ensure stability as server capacity is gradually scaled. The developers appreciate player patience, especially on the first day.
</Callout>

All progress made during this beta test will be wiped before the full game release. This is standard practice for beta phases to ensure a fair and fresh start for all players at launch.

### Revamped Combat and Movement

The developers have implemented several critical changes to the game's core mechanics, aiming for a more fluid and engaging experience:

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Movement System">
    Transitioned from tile-based to **fully free movement**, allowing for more dynamic positioning and evasion. Controller movement is particularly smooth, supporting 360° directionality.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Combat System">
    Shifted from a hybrid system to **full action combat**. Attacks are now entirely action-based, requiring players to aim skills with the mouse. Left-click is basic attack, right-click is special (hold for enhanced effect).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Control Scheme">
    Revamped mouse controls for better precision. Characters automatically face the mouse direction when attacking, simplifying targeting. Basic attacks now operate on a stamina-like system rather than strict cooldowns.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Hero Experience">
    With these changes, many of the 15 available heroes will feel distinctly different, offering a fresh experience even with familiar characters.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="tip" title="Mastering New Controls">
While keyboard and mouse controls are designed to feel easier overall, controller movement, though more fluid, may require some adjustment time. Practice aiming and movement to fully adapt to the new action combat system.
</Callout>

### Introducing PvP Modes

The Drakantos closed beta proudly unveils its PvP content, featuring the Arena with 13 maps across three distinct modes. This allows players to test their skills against others and explore competitive strategies.

| PvP Mode | Objective | Key Mechanics | Rewards |
|:---------|:----------|:--------------|:--------|
| **Control** | Capture and hold control zones to earn progress. First team to fully capture all zones wins. | Maps can have one or two control points; dominate all. Progress bar shows team and individual point status. | Proportional to match length; faster achievements due to shorter matches. |
| **Escort** | Escort your crystal to the final point while preventing the enemy from escorting theirs. | Crystal moves when allies are nearby, stops if enemies are close. Progress bar and minimap track crystal route. | Proportional to match length; faster achievements due to shorter matches. |
| **Assault** | MOBA-like mode: destroy enemy towers and core. | Towers share health, expose core when destroyed. Towers prioritize minions but target heroes attacking other heroes. Attack buff appears mid-map every 3:30. | Higher rewards due to longer match durations. |

<Callout type="info" title="PvP Itemization">
In PvP, all players are set to max level with maximized equipment and skills. Trophies and Artifacts are also maximized, even if not yet unlocked. Hold TAB while hovering over an item to see its max-level stats. Artifact charges are halved in Control and Escort modes.
</Callout>
