One of the most unique aspects of Drakantos, even in its beta phase, is its innovative hero system. Unlike traditional MMORPGs where you create a single character and choose a class, Drakantos allows you to freely swap between heroes, each representing a distinct playstyle.

In the Closed Beta, you'll have immediate access to 15 heroes, offering a wide range of combat roles and abilities. This encourages experimentation and allows players to find their preferred style without the need to create multiple characters.
