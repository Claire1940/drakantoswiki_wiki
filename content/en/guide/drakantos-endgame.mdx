### PvP Modes
The game features a variety of PvP modes, a ranked system, and regular in-game tournaments. This allows players to rise through the ranks and become champions within the community.

| PvP Mode | Description | Stat Balancing | Key Feature |
| :------- | :---------- | :------------- | :---------- |
| **Arena** | Structured 1v1 or team-based combat. | **Stats balanced** (gear doesn't affect stats). | Pure skill-based competition. |
| **Hellgate** | PvP + PvE area where players compete for resources. | Stats are *not* balanced (gear matters). | High-risk, high-reward open-world PvP. |
| **Tournaments** | Regular in-game events with competitive brackets. | Varies by tournament rules. | Prestigious rewards, community recognition. |

<Callout type="warning" title="PvP Stat Balancing">
In Arena PvP, your gear stats are balanced to ensure a competitive environment where only skill and strategy matter. However, in modes like Hellgate, gear will significantly impact your performance, so optimize your build accordingly.
</Callout>
