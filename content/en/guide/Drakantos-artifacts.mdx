<Section icon={Target} title="How to Optimize Artifact Synergy">

Choosing the right **Drakantos artifacts** depends heavily on your character's role. For instance, a support character like Faendar (the Archdruid) benefits immensely from artifacts that reduce cooldowns or increase the duration of crowd control effects like roots and slows. Since Faendar's ultimate, Helier's Blessing, is a gradual heal rather than a burst heal, artifacts that boost "Healing Over Time" (HoT) efficiency are mandatory for high-level content.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify Core Scaling">
    Determine if your character scales better with raw damage, cast speed, or utility. For Faendar, cast speed is vital for his basic attacks and Vine Grasp.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Utility Artifacts">
    Equip at least one artifact that provides defensive utility. The "Owl's Grace" artifact, for example, complements Faendar's movement ability by adding extra damage reduction.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Focus on Crowd Control">
    In 2026, the meta favors "Root" and "Slow" durations. Look for artifacts that extend the 3-second root of Deadly Thorns to maximize team damage windows.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Upgrade via Shards">
    Use the shards obtained from fishing and corrupted enemies to enhance the artifact's base stats at the town blacksmith.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Meta Strategy">
When playing Faendar, focus on artifacts that increase the health of your summoned Treants. Even though body blocking is removed for allies, Treants still block enemies, providing a strategic "wall" that can protect your backline.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Advanced Artifact Customization">

The depth of **Drakantos artifacts** goes beyond simple stat boosts. Each of the eight new artifacts features "Orb Slots" that allow players to customize specific skill behaviors. This system mirrors the ability orbs seen in the closed beta, where holding a skill could change its fundamental property—such as turning three small Treants into one massive, ground-smashing Ent.

By slotting specific artifact gems, you can further refine these "Perfect Timing" mechanics. For Faendar, this might mean increasing the attack reduction debuff applied by his Ent summon or reducing the required hold time for his Vine Grasp ability.
