<Section icon={Swords} title="Cosmetic Personalization Options">
While gear progression in Drakantos mainly focuses on power and statistics rather than visual changes, players still have avenues to customize their hero's appearance. These options are primarily cosmetic and allow for individual expression.

### Available Cosmetic Customization Elements

| Customization Aspect | Description | Impact on Appearance |
| :------------------- | :---------- | :------------------- |
| **Skins**            | Unique outfits and alternate forms for heroes. | Can drastically alter a hero's look (e.g., Mohazus' "living" version). |
| **Color Palettes**   | Dye systems to change the color scheme of outfits and accessories. | Allows for extensive personal color choices. |
| **Hairstyles**       | Different hair options for applicable heroes. | Provides variety in headwear and hair. |
| **Armor/Accessory Parts** | Cosmetic pieces like helmets, capes, and beards. | Adds unique flair to character models. |
| **Mounts & Pets**    | Companions and rides that follow or carry the character. | Enhances visual presence and adds personality. |
| **Emotes**           | Special animations for expressing emotions. | Affects character interaction and portrayal. |

<Callout type="info" title="Visual Progression Note">
Unlike many MMORPGs, the visual appearance of your character in Drakantos is not directly tied to the equipment you wear. Upgrading your armor or weapon will improve your stats, but your hero's look will remain the same unless you apply a specific cosmetic skin or dye.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Skill-Based Customization: Orbs, Artifacts, and Trophies">
Beyond cosmetics, Drakantos offers a robust system for customizing your hero's abilities and playstyle. This "build crafting" involves a combination of orbs, artifacts, and trophies, allowing players to tailor their hero's performance to their preferences.

### How Skill Customization Works

| Component  | Function                                    | Effect on Gameplay                                    |
| :--------- | :------------------------------------------ | :---------------------------------------------------- |
| **Orbs**   | Modify base skills of your hero.            | Enhance abilities, change their effects, or add new properties. |
| **Artifacts** | Add two active skills to your hero's toolkit. | Introduce new combat options and strategic depth.      |
| **Trophies** | Grant two passive skills.                   | Provide constant buffs or unique situational advantages. |

<Callout type="success" title="Deepening Your Build">
Experimenting with different combinations of orbs, artifacts, and trophies is crucial for finding a build that suits your playstyle. This system allows for significant strategic depth, even without traditional character creation.
</Callout>

While the game features pre-defined heroes, the skill customization system ensures that no two players of the same hero will necessarily play identically. For example, an orb might change a single-target attack into an AoE, or an artifact could grant a powerful new defensive ability. This allows for a dynamic and personalized combat experience.

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="The Hero-Based Approach vs. Traditional Character Creation">
The decision to use a hero-based system instead of traditional character creation is a deliberate design choice in Drakantos. This approach has both advantages and disadvantages for players seeking deep customization.

### Hero-Based vs. Traditional Creation

| Feature             | Hero-Based System (Drakantos)                | Traditional Character Creation (Typical MMORPGs) |
| :------------------ | :------------------------------------------- | :----------------------------------------------- |
| **Initial Choice**  | Select from a roster of pre-designed heroes. | Design appearance, choose race, class, gender.   |
| **Lore Integration** | Deep connection to hero's background and story. | Player defines their character's story.          |
| **Visual Variety**  | Relies on skins, dyes, and cosmetic parts.   | Gear often changes appearance; extensive sliders. |
| **Skill Customization** | Orbs, artifacts, trophies modify abilities. | Talent trees, skill points, gear stats.          |
| **Gender/Race Lock**| Heroes have fixed gender/race; skins might offer alternatives. | Player chooses gender/race freely.               |

<Callout type="warning" title="Player Expectations">
Players accustomed to extensive character creation in MMORPGs might find Drakantos's hero-based system initially restrictive. However, the focus shifts to mastering a hero's unique toolkit and customizing their abilities.
</Callout>

The developers acknowledge that some players might prefer the freedom of creating an avatar from scratch. However, their vision prioritizes the narrative depth and distinct identity of each hero. This allows them to craft more intricate backstories and personalities that are directly woven into the game's world and quests.

</Section>
