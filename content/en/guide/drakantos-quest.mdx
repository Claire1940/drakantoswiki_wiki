<Section icon={Target} title="The Lost Donkey Quest: Step-by-Step">
The "Find the Lost Donkey" quest is your gateway to acquiring your first mount. This quest involves several distinct phases, from locating the animal to taming and finally licensing it for use.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate the Quest with Sally">
    Head to the stable in town and speak with Sally. She will task you with finding her lost donkey. Her location is usually near the stable building.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate the Lost Donkey">
    The donkey's general location will be marked by a search circle on your map. Specifically, you'll find it in the **southern part** of this circle, often at an intersection.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Guide the Donkey Back">
    Once found, approach the donkey. It will follow you if you stay close. Guide it north towards the town and ensure it enters the stable.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Discuss Mount Options with Sally">
    Return to Sally, who will now be at the bottom of the stable area. Talk to her and express your desire for a mount, specifically the donkey you just found.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Tame the Donkey">
    Sally will then present a mini-game to tame the donkey. Throw rocks to scare it and prevent it from evading, then use a rope. When the donkey is not resisting, hold the designated button (e.g., LB on a controller) to complete the taming process.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Obtain Your Mount License">
    After taming, Sally will inform you that you need a license to ride. You must race against her to earn this license. The first race is generally straightforward; use your boost strategically and follow the flags to the finish line.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Quest Complete!">
Upon successfully completing the race and having tamed the donkey, you will receive your mount license. You can now ride your new donkey!
</Callout>

</Section>
