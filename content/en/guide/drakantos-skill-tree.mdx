<Section icon={Trophy} title="Understanding Orbs: The Core of Ability Customization">
Orbs are perhaps the most impactful element in customizing your hero's abilities in Drakantos. These powerful items can fundamentally change how a skill behaves, adding new effects, enhancing existing ones, or even completely transforming an ability into something entirely new. This offers an incredible degree of flexibility, allowing players to adapt their hero to various combat scenarios or personal preferences.

| Orb Effect Category | Description | Example Impact |
|:--------------------|:------------|:---------------|
| **Transformation**  | Changes an ability's core function. | *Liam's Giant Slash* becomes a ranged projectile. |
| **Enhancement**     | Boosts existing stats or adds minor effects. | *Iris's Magic Missile* gains a chance to stun. |
| **Utility Additions** | Incorporates new utility to an ability. | *Ozul's Sinister Strike* now applies a bleed effect. |
| **Elemental Shift** | Alters an ability's damage type or associated effects. | *Byron's Fireball* can gain a frost component. |

For example, a specific Orb might cause a single-target attack to become an area-of-effect spell, or imbue a defensive skill with an offensive counter-attack. The possibilities are vast, encouraging players to collect and try out various Orbs to find their ideal setups.

<Callout type="success" title="Strategic Orb Farming">

<Section icon={Shield} title="Artifacts: Account-Wide Power Boosts">
Artifacts in Drakantos provide significant power boosts and are a crucial component of hero customization. Each hero can equip two artifacts. A key feature of artifacts is that many of them are account-wide, meaning once unlocked, they can be used by almost any hero on your account. This makes them a valuable investment, as they contribute to the overall strength and versatility of your entire roster.

| Artifact Type | Effect Example | Usage Flexibility | Restrictions |
|:--------------|:---------------|:------------------|:-------------|
| **Damage Boost** | Increases critical hit chance or damage. | Account-wide | May be limited by faction or element. |
| **Defensive** | Grants damage reduction or shield generation. | Account-wide | Some artifacts are hero-specific. |
| **Utility** | Reduces cooldowns or boosts movement speed. | Account-wide | Very few are hero-specific. |
| **Elemental** | Enhances specific elemental damage or resistance. | Account-wide | Typically tied to specific elements. |

While most artifacts are account-wide, some may have restrictions based on faction, elemental alignment, or even be exclusive to certain heroes. However, the sheer variety ensures that you'll find plenty of artifacts applicable across multiple characters, promoting diverse build crafting without requiring endless grinding for each hero.

<Callout type="info" title="Account-Wide Advantage">
Leverage the account-wide nature of many Artifacts to quickly power up new heroes or experiment with different roles, as you won't need to re-farm them for every character.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Relics: Game-Changing Passives">
Relics, often referred to as Trophies, occupy two dedicated slots on your hero and provide powerful passive abilities. These aren't just minor stat boosts; many relics have the potential to completely change your gameplay style, offering unique strategic advantages or altering how your hero interacts with the environment and enemies.

| Relic Type | Passive Effect Example | Gameplay Impact | Acquisition Method |
|:-----------|:-----------------------|:----------------|:-------------------|
| **Defensive** | Grants periodic invulnerability or shield. | Enhances survivability, allows for aggressive plays. | High-tier dungeons, specific quests. |
| **Offensive** | Automatically triggers an ability on critical hit. | Increases damage output, creates dynamic combat. | Boss drops, PvP rewards. |
| **Support** | Heals nearby allies on ability use. | Boosts team utility, crucial for healer builds. | Adventurer's Guild missions. |
| **Utility** | Increases resource generation or movement speed. | Improves exploration, facilitates faster progression. | Exploration rewards, side quests. |

The careful selection of relics is paramount for optimizing your hero's performance and defining your unique playstyle in Drakantos. They are often sought-after endgame items that can truly differentiate one player's build from another.

<Callout type="warning" title="Relic Rarity">
Powerful relics are often rare drops from challenging content. Be prepared for a dedicated grind to acquire the most impactful passive bonuses for your desired build.

The Drakantos build system is designed to offer deep customization without the constraints of a traditional **Drakantos skill tree**. | Component | Primary Function | Customization Impact | Synergy Potential |

| **Orbs** | Modify or transform individual abilities. | High impact on skill behavior. | Direct interaction with hero abilities. |
| **Artifacts** | Account-wide stat boosts and unique effects. | Broaden hero versatility. | Can enhance specific elements or playstyles across multiple heroes. |
| **Relics (Trophies)** | Powerful passive bonuses, gameplay alterations. | Significant changes to combat flow. | Can define a hero's unique combat identity. |
| **Equipment** | Standard stat increases, set bonuses. | Fundamental power increase. | Complements other components, providing base stats. |

This interconnected system ensures that players are constantly engaged in optimizing their builds, searching for the perfect combination of items to suit their preferred playstyle and overcome game challenges.
