<Section icon={Target} title="How to Use Map Markers and Auto-Pathing">

Efficiency is key in any MMORPG, and the **Drakantos interactive map** offers one of the most robust auto-pathing systems in the pixel-art genre. This system allows you to focus on managing your inventory or chatting with guildmates while your character travels.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify Your Destination">
    Open the world map (M) and locate the icon for your desired destination. Icons are color-coded: Yellow for quests, Blue for services, and Red for combat zones.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Initiate Auto-Pathing">
    Simply left-click on the map location. A path will be drawn on the mini-map, and your hero will begin moving toward the target automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Monitor for Obstacles">
    While auto-pathing is reliable, be wary of "body-blocking" from NPCs or other players in crowded areas. The developers are currently refining the pathfinding to minimize these collisions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Arrival and Interaction">
    Once you arrive, the auto-pathing will cease. You can then interact with NPCs or enter instanced adventures directly from the map interface.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The auto-pathing system is particularly useful when navigating the massive city of Casteron. Because the city layout is complex, using the map to find specific vendors like the Blacksmith or the Alchemist saves significant time.

<Callout type="success" title="Exploration Reward">
Discovering new areas on the map often grants "Adventurer XP," which contributes to your overall account level and unlocks new tiers of missions.
</Callout>
