<Section icon={Swords} title="Core Components of a Drakantos Build">
Creating an optimal **Drakantos build** revolves around three primary elements: Orbs, Artifacts, and Skill Customization. Each element plays a crucial role in defining your hero's strengths and weaknesses, and understanding their synergy is key to success.

### Orbs
Orbs are powerful enhancements that can modify a hero's abilities. They are a fundamental part of tailoring your hero to specific roles or playstyles.

| Orb Type    | Primary Effect                               | Secondary Effect                               | Recommended Use Case                               |
| :---------- | :------------------------------------------- | :--------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------- |
| **Attack**  | Increases damage output                      | Enhances critical strike chance                | High damage dealers, offensive playstyles          |
| **Defense** | Boosts health and armor                      | Reduces incoming damage                        | Tanks, frontline heroes, survivability focus       |
| **Utility** | Provides crowd control, movement speed       | Reduces cooldowns, adds support effects        | Support heroes, tactical play, team utility        |
| **Hybrid**  | Balanced offense and defense                 | Offers minor boosts to multiple stats          | Versatile builds, adaptable roles                  |

<Callout type="warning" title="Orb Application Warning">
When opening orb boxes, ensure you apply them to your intended hero. Orbs are tied to the character they are used on and cannot be easily transferred to an alt. This is crucial for optimizing your **Drakantos build**.
</Callout>

### Artifacts
Artifacts provide unique bonuses and passive effects that can significantly alter a hero's performance. They often complement orbs and skills to create powerful combinations.

| Artifact Type | Effect Category                          | Common Bonuses                                     | Strategic Impact                                   |
| :------------ | :--------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------- |
| **Relic**     | Core stat enhancement                    | % stat increase (e.g., Attack, Health, Mana)       | Foundation of any build, consistent power boost    |
| **Gear**      | Ability modification                     | Skill damage increase, range extension, new effects | Modifies hero's core abilities, creates new combos |
| **Trophy**    | Situational buffs                        | On-kill effects, damage reduction at low HP        | Enhances specific play moments, comeback potential  |

### Skill Customization
**Drakantos build** flexibility is further expanded through skill customization. Each hero has unique abilities that can be modified with orbs and artifacts, allowing for diverse gameplay styles even with the same character. This system breaks away from rigid class roles, encouraging players to experiment.

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Crafting Your Ideal Drakantos Build">
Developing a powerful **Drakantos build** requires a systematic approach, especially with the game's new action combat system and freeform movement. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you optimize your hero.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Understand Your Hero's Base Kit">
    Familiarize yourself with your chosen hero's fundamental abilities, their range, cooldowns, and damage types. The new combat system means heroes may feel different from previous beta tests, so re-evaluate their core mechanics. Consider if they are primarily melee or ranged, and what their primary damage source is.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Define Your Role and Playstyle">
    Decide whether you want to focus on damage, tanking, support, or a hybrid role. Are you aggressive, defensive, or tactical? Your chosen role will guide your orb and artifact selections. For instance, an aggressive PvP player might prioritize Attack Orbs and Gear Artifacts that enhance skill damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Select Orbs for Synergy">
    Choose orbs that complement your hero's base kit and your desired playstyle. If your hero has strong area-of-effect (AoE) skills, consider orbs that boost AoE damage or reduce their cooldowns. For a single-target burst damage hero, focus on critical strike or raw damage boosts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Equip Artifacts Strategically">
    Artifacts often provide unique effects that can drastically change a build. Look for artifacts that synergize with your selected orbs and skills. For example, if you have orbs that increase attack speed, an artifact that triggers an effect on a certain number of basic attacks could be very powerful. Remember that in PvP, artifact charges are often reduced.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Test and Refine in PvP/PvE">
    The beta boxes provide an excellent opportunity to test various builds. Don't be afraid to experiment. Play through PvE content to understand basic functionality, then jump into PvP modes like Control, Escort, or Assault to see how your build performs against other players. Adjust your orb and artifact choices based on performance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="PvP Build Advantage">
In PvP modes, all characters are set to max level with max equipment and skills. This creates a fair playing field where your **Drakantos build** choices and strategic execution matter most, rather than gear grind. Focus on skill synergy and counter-building.
</Callout>
