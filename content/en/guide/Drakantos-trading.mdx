### Best Practices for Selling Items

Follow these steps to ensure your items sell quickly and for a good price:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Research Current Prices">
    Before listing, check what similar items are currently selling for. Look at both active listings and recent sales history if available to gauge a fair price.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Price Competitively">
    If you want a quick sale, price slightly below the competition. For maximum profit, you might need to wait longer, but ensure your price isn't significantly higher than others.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Provide Clear Descriptions">
    For unique or customized items, highlight their key stats or benefits in the description. This helps potential buyers understand the value.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Relist Regularly">
    Items can get buried under new listings. If an item hasn't sold, consider relisting it after a period, perhaps adjusting the price.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Advanced Trading Techniques

Consider these methods to get ahead in the **Drakantos trading** game:

| Technique         | Description                                                                 | Risk Level | Potential Reward |
|-------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------|------------|------------------|
| **Arbitrage**     | Buying low in one market (or at off-peak times) and selling high in another (or at peak times). | Medium     | High             |
| **Bulk Buying/Selling** | Purchasing large quantities of commonly used items (e.g., crafting materials) when they are cheap, then selling them in smaller, more profitable stacks. | Low        | Medium           |
| **Crafting & Selling** | Acquiring raw materials, crafting high-demand items, and selling the finished products for a profit. Requires crafting skill. | Medium     | High             |
| **Speculation**   | Buying items you anticipate will increase in value due to future game updates, events, or meta changes. | High       | Very High        |
| **Flipping**      | Buying undervalued items and immediately reselling them at their true market value. | Low        | Medium           |
