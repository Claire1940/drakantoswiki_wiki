<Section icon={Shield} title="Marly's Defensive and Basic Drakantos Skills">
Marly's primary defensive ability, Divine Protection, is a cornerstone of her kit. Her basic attack, Holy Light, provides consistent damage. These skills, when enhanced with orbs, can offer much more than their base functions.

### Divine Protection
Marly's defensive ability, Divine Protection, casts a shield on herself, absorbing damage. Orb modifications can dramatically increase its utility.

| Orb Level | Effect | Description |
| :-------- | :----- | :---------- |
| Base      | Shield equal to 20% of max health | Protects Marly until broken or reactivated. |
| Orb 1     | +30% Damage Reduction | Reduces incoming damage while the shield is active. |
| Orb 2     | Target Ally | Allows Marly to cast the shield on a selected ally. |
| Orb 3     | Shield Break Blast | Releases a holy light blast (106 damage) and knocks back nearby enemies when the shield breaks. |
| Orb 4     | 4-second Invulnerability | Grants invulnerability at the start of casting for 4 seconds. |

### Holy Light (Basic Attack)
Marly's basic attack, Holy Light, is a straightforward light bolt that deals damage. Its orb modifications focus on improving its cast speed.

| Orb Level | Effect | Description |
| :-------- | :----- | :---------- |
| Base      | 42 Damage | Shoots a light bolt at the target. |
| Orb 1     | +10% Cast Speed | Increases the speed at which Holy Light is cast. |
| Orb 2     | +25% Cast Speed | Further increases cast speed, for faster attacks. |

<Section icon={Swords} title="Offensive and Area-of-Effect Drakantos Skills">
Marly also possesses abilities that allow her to contribute offensively and control crowds, such as Chastise and Holy Nova. These skills are essential for managing enemy groups and supporting her allies in combat.

### Chastise
Chastise is a frontal area-of-effect (AoE) attack that roots enemies, providing crucial crowd control.

| Orb Level | Effect | Description |
| :-------- | :----- | :---------- |
| Base      | 89 Damage, 1-second Root | Deals damage in a frontal area and roots enemies. |
| Orb 1     | Charge for More Damage | Holding the ability charges it for increased damage. |
| Orb 2     | Charge for Longer Root | Charging also increases the duration of the root effect. |
| Orb 3     | Reduced Charge Time | Decreases the time required to fully charge Chastise. |

### Holy Nova
Holy Nova is a powerful AoE ability that damages enemies around Marly and slows them down. Its orb enhancements can turn it into a significant team utility.

| Orb Level | Effect | Description |
| :-------- | :----- | :---------- |
| Base      | AoE Damage, 60% Movement Slow (4s) | Damages all nearby enemies and slows their movement speed. |
| Orb 1     | Charge for Ally Shield | Charging casts Divine Protection on nearby allies, granting a 20% max health shield for 15 seconds. |
| Orb 2     | Larger Area, Knockback | Increases the blast radius and adds a knockback effect, great for crowd control. |

<Callout type="success" title="Crowd Control Synergy">
Combining Chastise's root with Holy Nova's slow and potential knockback allows Marly to effectively control large groups of enemies, creating opportunities for her teammates to deal damage or reposition. This synergy is a key aspect of her **Drakantos skills** toolkit.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Ultimate Support and Healing Abilities">
Marly's ultimate abilities, Guardian Light and Seriel's Light, solidify her role as a dedicated support character. These skills provide critical protection and sustained healing, ensuring her allies survive the toughest encounters.

### Guardian Light
Guardian Light is a single-target defensive ability that grants temporary invulnerability and then explodes, damaging nearby enemies.

| Orb Level | Effect | Description |
| :-------- | :----- | :---------- |
| Base      | 100% Damage Reduction (2s), Explosion | Grants selected ally 100% damage reduction for 2 seconds, then explodes. |
| Orb 1     | Removes Slows, +Movement Speed | Removes slow effects and increases the ally's movement speed during the 2 seconds. |
| Orb 2     | Shield for Each Enemy Hit | Grants a shield equal to 5% of Marly's max health for each enemy hit by the explosion, lasting 4 seconds. |

### Seriel's Light
Seriel's Light is Marly's ultimate healing ability, providing continuous healing over an area for a duration.

| Orb Level | Effect | Description |
| :-------- | :----- | :---------- |
| Base      | Continuous Healing (10s) | Places an area that provides continuous healing, stronger at the start and gradually diminishing. |
| Orb 1     | Increased Healing Radius | Expands the area of effect for Seriel's Light, healing more allies. |
| Orb 2     | Healing over Time Buff | Allies leaving the area continue to receive a small heal over time buff for 5 seconds. |

<Callout type="info" title="Strategic Placement">
The effectiveness of Seriel's Light heavily relies on **strategic placement**. Positioning it where allies can benefit from its sustained healing, especially during prolonged engagements, can turn the tide of battle. Consider choke points or areas where your team will be grouped for maximum impact.
</Callout>
