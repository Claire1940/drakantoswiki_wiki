<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Orbs: Enhancing Your Abilities">
Orbs are unique **Drakantos items** that allow for significant skill customization. Unlike traditional gear, Orbs are slotted directly into your hero's abilities, boosting their effectiveness and sometimes even altering their mechanics. This system enables players to tailor their skills to their preferred playstyle, whether focusing on damage, defense, or utility.

When you find an Orb, it corresponds to a specific skill. Once used, it permanently enhances that skill for the character it was applied to. This means that if you switch to a different hero, you'll need to find and apply Orbs relevant to their skills as well. The strategic placement of Orbs can dramatically change how a hero performs in combat, making them a cornerstone of character building.

<Callout type="warning" title="Orb Allocation is Permanent">
Once an Orb is slotted into a skill, it cannot be transferred to another character or removed. Choose wisely which hero and skill receive the Orb to optimize your build.
</Callout>

| **Offensive Skill** | More damage, wider AoE, additional debuffs | Higher burst damage, improved mob clearing, boss DPS |

<Section icon={Swords} title="Artifacts: Game-Changing Active Skills">
Artifacts are another critical type of **Drakantos items** that offer powerful active abilities, essentially providing your hero with additional skills. You can equip two Artifacts at a time, and these abilities refresh every time you enter or exit a dungeon. This allows for dynamic playstyle changes and tactical flexibility depending on the dungeon or PvP encounter you're facing.

Artifacts can range from healing items and damage-over-time effects to defensive buffs and crowd control. For example, some Artifacts might make enemies attack each other, while others summon damaging beehives. Their availability makes them highly impactful for adapting to different combat scenarios and filling gaps in your hero's natural skillset.

<Callout type="success" title="Artifact Synergy">
Experiment with different combinations of Artifacts to find synergies that complement your hero's abilities. A well-chosen Artifact duo can significantly boost your effectiveness in combat.
</Callout>
