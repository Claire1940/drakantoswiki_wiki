<Section icon={Target} title="Solo Progression Step-by-Step">

Advancing through the world of Drakantos alone requires a disciplined approach to power levels and questing. The open world is vast and interlaced with loading sections, requiring players to balance main story quests with dungeon grinding to stay ahead of the difficulty curve.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Complete the Prologue">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlock the Adventurers Guild">
    Push through the early open-world dialogue to unlock the Guild. This allows you to queue for dungeons directly from your inventory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Grind Power Level 460">
    Focus on "stat stick" gear progression. Equip any items that increase your base power level to unlock access to the first set of dungeons.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Master Artifact Orbs">

  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Reach the Level 12 Milestone">

  </Step>
