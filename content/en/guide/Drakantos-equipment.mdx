The heart of **Drakantos equipment** customization lies in three distinct item types. These items allow you to transform a standard hero into a specialized powerhouse. Because Drakantos does not use a traditional class system, these items are the only way to differentiate your build from other players using the same hero.

  <Card title="Orbs">

Artifacts are particularly impactful during the leveling process. For instance, a melee hero struggling with kiting can equip a ranged artifact to deal damage while maintaining distance. Orbs, on the other hand, are the key to high-level mastery. By modifying your hero's cooldowns or area-of-effect (AoE) properties, you can optimize for solo play or group synergy.

| Item Type | Rarity | Scaling Impact | Customization Depth |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Common Orb** | Low | Minor | Basic skill tweaks |
| **Rare Artifact** | Medium | High | Adds one active ability |
| **Epic Trophy** | High | Extreme | Significant stat multipliers |
| **Legendary Box** | Very High | Maximum | Choice of top-tier endgame gear |

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Orb Rule">
When opening an Orb Box, always ensure you are currently playing the hero you intend to gear up. One of the three options in an Orb Box is guaranteed to be for your active hero, preventing wasted pulls on characters you don't use.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Equipment Progression">

Navigating the early game requires a disciplined approach to upgrading your **Drakantos equipment**. The transition from the starter village to the major city of Casteron is gated by your ability to manage resources and complete specific tasks for NPCs like Marcelo the blacksmith.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Complete Initial Adventurers Guild Tasks">
    Follow the main story until you reach the Adventurers Guild. Speak to the Guild Master and complete the first few introductory missions to receive your basic gear set.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Visit Marcelo's Forge">
    Marcelo provides your first artifact—often a hand crossbow. This item is essential for increasing your early-game damage and reaching the initial Power Level requirements.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Reach Power Level 460">
    Grind Adventure Rank 1 missions. Focus on missions that offer "Possible Rewards" matching your lowest-level gear slots. Once you hit 460, the game world expands significantly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unlock Hero Abilities">
    Equipment isn't just about stats; it's about synergy. As you level up (milestones at levels 4, 8, and 12), ensure your artifacts complement your newly unlocked hero abilities.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Progression Phase | Target Power Level | Key Unlock | Recommended Activity |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Early Game** | 0 - 460 | Mount Quest | Rank 1 Adventures |
| **Mid Game** | 460 - 1200 | Casteron City | Rank 2 Missions, Side Quests |
| **Endgame** | 1200+ | PvP Arenas | Ranked PvP, Elite Trophies |

<Callout type="success" title="Progression Tip">
If you find a mission too difficult due to enemy scaling, try running it solo. While Drakantos is a multiplayer game, soloing adventures prevents enemies from receiving the health and damage buffs associated with group play.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="PvP Equipment and Scaling Mechanics">

Drakantos features a robust PvP system that handles **Drakantos equipment** differently than the PvE mode. To ensure a level playing field, the game utilizes a scaling system in the Arena, which features modes like Control, Escort, and Assault (MOBA-style).

In PvP, all players are set to the maximum level with maximized equipment and skills. However, the choice of Artifacts and Trophies still matters, as these items are not normalized in terms of their unique effects, only their raw stats. This allows for diverse builds without the "gear gap" often found in other MMORPGs.

| Feature | PvE Mode | PvP Arena |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Stat Scaling** | Based on gear level | Fully normalized to Max |
| **Artifact Use** | Full charges available | Charges reduced by 50% |
| **Ability Effects** | Full duration | Reduced duration (CC balance) |
| **Progression** | Gear drops & XP | Beta Boxes & Ranking |

<Callout type="tip" title="PvP Build Strategy">
Since stats are even in PvP, focus your Trophy and Artifact selection on utility and crowd control (CC). Items that provide stuns, slows, or displacement are far more valuable in the Arena than pure damage modifiers.
</Callout>
