Drakantos dungeons are instanced missions designed for cooperative play, though solo options are also available. These adventures are dynamic, meaning their enemies, bosses, resources, traps, and objectives can change over time. This variability ensures that each playthrough remains unique and challenging, preventing monotony and encouraging adaptability.

### Step-by-Step Dungeon Entry

Entering a dungeon in Drakantos is straightforward. Follow these steps to begin your adventure:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Adventure Mode Interface">
    From the main menu or a town hub, find the "Adventure Mode" option. This is typically where you queue for PvE instances.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Your Desired Adventure">
    Browse the available dungeons. Each will display its recommended power level and potential rewards. Choose one that matches your current character strength.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Choose Difficulty Tier">
    If multiple difficulty tiers are available for the selected dungeon, pick the one you wish to attempt. Higher tiers offer better rewards but present greater challenges.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Queue Up (Solo or Party)">
    Decide whether to enter solo, or queue with a pre-made party. You can also use the matchmaking system to find teammates.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Prepare for Battle">
    Once the queue pops, confirm your entry. Ensure your hero is properly equipped with the best gear and artifacts, and that your abilities are set for the upcoming challenge.
  </Step>
</Steps>
