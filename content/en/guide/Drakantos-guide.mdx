<Section icon={Shield} title="Progression Systems: Gear Score and Orbs">

Progression in Drakantos is a two-pronged approach: leveling your hero and increasing your **Gear Score**. Gear Score is the primary gatekeeper for content; even a high-level hero will struggle in advanced dungeons if their equipment is outdated.

**The Gear System**
Your character equips four main pieces: a Hat, Top, Shoes, and a Weapon. These items are found in dungeons and can be reinforced at the Blacksmith using materials gathered in the open world (like wood and ore).

**Orbs and Skill Boosting**
Orbs are unique items found during Adventures that allow you to permanently enhance specific skills. For example, a "Heavy Attack Orb" might allow you to hold down your basic attack button for a massive damage boost.

| Progression Item | Source | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Gear** | Dungeons (Adventures) | Increases base stats and Gear Score |
| **Materials** | Open World Gathering | Used to reinforce and upgrade gear |
| **Orbs** | Rare Dungeon Drops | Permanently modifies and buffs skills |
| **Artifacts** | Bosses / Beta Boxes | Grants unique active abilities |

<Section icon={Target} title="PvE Adventure Mode: The Gameplay Loop">

The heart of Drakantos is the **Adventure Mode**. These are 3-man instanced dungeons that rotate every 20-30 minutes. Each dungeon has specific objectives and loot tables, allowing you to target-farm the materials you need.

**Common Dungeon Objectives:**
- **Exterminate**: Kill a set percentage of mobs in the area.
- **Escort**: Protect an NPC or object as it moves across the map.
- **Collect**: Find rare ingredients or remnants scattered throughout the zone.
- **Boss Hunt**: Track down and defeat a powerful champion at the end of the cave.

| Objective Type | Best Strategy | Common Rewards |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Exterminate** | Group mobs for AoE damage | Experience and Gold |
| **Escort** | Tank stays near objective, DPS clears path | Upgrade Materials |
| **Collect** | Split up to find items faster | Rare Crafting Components |
| **Boss Hunt** | Focus on dodging telegraphs and CC | High-tier Gear and Orbs |

<Section icon={Trophy} title="PvP Arena: Modes and Mechanics">

For players who prefer competition, Drakantos offers a robust PvP Arena system. In the Arena, all players are set to maximum level with normalized equipment to ensure a fair playing field. In 2026, there are three primary modes across 13 different maps.

**PvP Modes Breakdown:**
1. **Control**: Teams fight to hold specific zones. Progress is earned by standing in the zone, even if enemies are present.
2. **Escort**: A tug-of-war style mode where you must move a crystal to the enemy base while stopping theirs.
3. **Assault**: A MOBA-inspired mode featuring towers and a central core. Towers prioritize minions but will target heroes who attack allies under the tower.

| PvP Mode | Objective | Key Mechanic |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Control** | Capture 1-2 zones | First team to 100% progress wins |
| **Escort** | Move crystal to destination | Crystal stops if enemies are nearby |
| **Assault** | Destroy the enemy core | Towers have 80% defense without minions |

<Callout type="tip" title="PvP Strategy">
In **Assault mode**, a powerful damage buff appears in the center of the map every 3:30. Securing this 25% attack boost is often the key to breaking through enemy tower defenses.
</Callout>
