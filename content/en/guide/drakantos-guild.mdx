<Section icon={Target} title="Forming Your Drakantos Guild: A Step-by-Step Guide">
Establishing a guild in Drakantos involves several key steps, from initial creation to building a strong foundation. A well-organized guild is crucial for attracting members and achieving collective goals.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Guild Creation">
    Locate the Guild Manager in a major city (e.g., Akamond or Kasteron). You'll typically need to complete a small quest or pay a fee to register your guild. Choose a unique and memorable guild name.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Define Your Guild's Vision">
    Decide on your guild's primary focus: PvE (dungeons, quests), PvP (tournaments, ranked play), or a balanced approach. This vision will guide your recruitment efforts and internal policies.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Recruitment Strategy">
    Actively recruit members who align with your guild's vision. Utilize in-game chat, official forums, and community platforms. Clearly state your requirements and expectations for new recruits.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Establish Guild Structure">
    Assign roles and responsibilities to key members (e.g., officers, recruiters, raid leaders). A clear hierarchy helps with organization and decision-making.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Set Up Guild Housing & Bank">
    Utilize the guild housing feature to create a central hub for your members. Establish a guild bank or shared storage system for resources and valuable items to be contributed and utilized by the guild.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Early Game Guilds">
