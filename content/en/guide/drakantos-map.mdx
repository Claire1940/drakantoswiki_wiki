### How to Get Your First Mount (Donkey)

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Speak to Sally">
    Head to the stable in town and locate Sally. She will initiate the "Find the Lost Donkey" quest.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate the Lost Donkey">
    The lost Donkey is typically found in the southern part of the marked search area on your **Drakantos map**, often near an intersection.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Guide the Donkey Home">
    Once you find the Donkey, stay close to it, and it will follow you. Guide it back north to the stable in town. Ensure the Donkey enters the stable to complete this part of the mission.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Tame the Donkey">
    Talk to Sally again. She will ask you to tame the Donkey. This involves a mini-game where you use a rock to scare the Donkey and prevent it from evading your rope. When it's not resisting, hold the specified button (e.g., LB) to tame it.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Obtain Your Mount License">
    After taming, speak to Sally one more time. You'll need a license to ride. Participate in a simple race against her, following flags to the finish line. Use your boost strategically. Winning the race grants you the mount license.
  </Step>
</Steps>
