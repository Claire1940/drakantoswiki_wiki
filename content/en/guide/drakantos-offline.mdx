<Section icon={Map} title="Drakantos: An Exclusively Online Experience">
Drakantos, developed by Wingeon Game Studios LLC, is designed from the ground up as a Massively Multiplayer Online Role-Playing Game (MMORPG). This means that, unlike many traditional RPGs, an **internet connection is required to play** at all times. There is no **Drakantos offline** mode available, even for single-player content or story progression. This design choice underpins many of the game's core features and its overall player experience.

<Section icon={Target} title="Minimum System Requirements and Connectivity">
To ensure players are prepared, the official Steam page for Drakantos clearly outlines the system requirements, specifically mentioning the need for an internet connection.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Check Operating System">
    Ensure your system runs **Windows 7+** or **Linux 64 bit**. These are the supported operating systems for Drakantos.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Verify Hardware Specifications">
    Confirm you meet the minimum hardware: an **Intel Core i3 processor**, **4 GB RAM**, and **1GB Video Memory (shader model 3.0+)**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Ensure Internet Connection">
    Critically, confirm you have a **broadband internet connection**. This is explicitly stated as "Internet connection required to play" for both Windows and Linux.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Allocate Storage Space">
    Make sure you have at least **1 GB of available storage space** for the game installation.
  </Step>
</Steps>
