<Section icon={Bolt} title="Hero Classes and Combat Dynamics">

Drakantos features an evolving roster of heroes, each offering a distinct playstyle. Unlike traditional MMORPGs with fixed classes, Drakantos heroes have unique mechanics and roles that bring new dynamics to cooperative gameplay. Players can experiment with various heroes like Knight Priestess, Paladin, Stormlancer, Ronin, Monk, Bard, and more, each with their own set of skills.

Combat involves using a hotbar of abilities, with new skills unlocking at specific level milestones. While early playtests highlighted some concerns about combat fluidity, such as animation locks and basic attack cooldowns, developers have acknowledged these issues and are working on improvements. The goal is to ensure combat feels engaging and responsive, especially for ranged DPS characters who need to kite effectively.
