Drakantos gameplay revolves around its diverse hero roster and cooperative dungeon adventures. Each hero brings a unique set of skills and a distinct playstyle. ### Early Combat Feedback and Developer Response

Initial impressions from the playtest highlighted several areas for improvement in combat. Players noted that every ability, including basic attacks, felt "animation locked," leading to a clunky and less fluid combat experience. The inability to attack diagonally and the presence of cooldowns on basic attacks were also points of concern, particularly for ranged DPS characters who found kiting difficult.

<Callout type="warning" title="Addressing Playtest Concerns">
The developers have acknowledged player feedback regarding combat fluidity, animation locking, and basic attack cooldowns. They are actively working on improvements, including fixes for movement responsiveness and NPC body blocking, to enhance the overall gameplay experience for the full release.
</Callout>

The development team has been responsive, already implementing changes such as reducing the level requirements for new abilities (from levels 5, 10, 15 to 4, 8, 12). This demonstrates their commitment to refining the game based on community input.

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Community and Multiplayer Experience">
Drakantos is designed as a massively multiplayer online RPG, emphasizing interaction and cooperation. The game features various avenues for players to connect, from cooperative dungeon delves to competitive PvP battles and robust guild systems.

### Cooperative Adventures and Social Features

Players will frequently group with others for adventures, tackling objectives and sharing rewards. While the multiplayer aspect is core, some playtesters expressed a desire for solo options or the ability to control grouping preferences, as unexpected teammates could sometimes scale up difficulty or alter the pace.

<Callout type="info" title="Multiplayer Scaling">
Drakantos features dynamic scaling for adventures. If additional players join an ongoing mission, enemy strength increases proportionally to maintain a challenge. This ensures that content remains engaging whether you're playing with one or multiple companions.
</Callout>

The game also includes a fully player-driven economy through its market, allowing for trade of rare items and collectibles. Guilds offer a structured way for players to team up, pursue common goals, and strive for dominance on the continent. Real-time voice and text chat are crucial for building networks and navigating rivalries within player-run clans.

### PvP and Competitive Play

For those seeking to test their skills, Drakantos offers a dedicated PvP system with various game modes, a ranked system, and regular in-game tournaments. The developers aim to ensure a competitive environment where skill and strategy matter most, with stats being evened out in PvP scenarios.
