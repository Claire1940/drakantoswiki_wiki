<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Anticipated Mobile Experience and Optimization">
If Drakantos makes the leap to mobile, optimizing the experience for smaller screens and touch controls will be crucial. Given the game's pixel art aesthetic and top-down perspective, it's well-suited for mobile adaptation. The current keyboard-based combat system on PC would need a streamlined touch interface, potentially with virtual joysticks and easily accessible ability buttons.

The developers have already demonstrated responsiveness to player feedback regarding gameplay fluidity and controls during PC playtests. This bodes well for a potential mobile version, as it suggests a commitment to ensuring a smooth and enjoyable experience across all platforms.
